Who We Are:

A motor scooter rental company located on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea.

We are an owner-operated company that caters to locals, walk-in tourists, motor scooter enthusiasts, and cruise-based touring groups and partnerships.

We are a friendly and easy to rent service of motor scooters, jeeps and side-by-sides (ATVs).
