Audrey Checketts

Contrast

Bluey

bluey.tv

Bluey shows great contrast with its bright colors, distinct lettering, fun images and backgrounds.

Hick's Law

Smith's

smithsfoodanddrug.com

Smith's demonstrates Hick's law by having an organized menu that allows the user to chose categories and search for specific information but not feel overwhelmed.

Fitt's Law

Cody Fry

codyfry.com

Cody Fry's website has a button close to the action element, latest release, and is easy to see. The user is able to click the button to listen or purchase his music. They don't have to search all over the website for it.
